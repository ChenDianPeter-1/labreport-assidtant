We report a real time study of the enthalpy release and heat capacity during the course of HCl-catalyzed hydrolysis of sucrose to fructose and glucose. Measurements were performed during both isothermal conditions and during slow heating and then cooling at a controlled rate. The reaction rate constant of the first-order kinetics follows an Arrhenius relation with activation energy of 109.2 kJ/mol of sucrose. On hydrolysis, the enthalpy decreases by 14.4 kJ/mol of sucrose at 310 K, and the heat capacity, Cp, increases by 61 J mol-1 K-1 of sucrose in the solution. The enthalpy of hydrolysis decreases with increase in the temperature and ΔCp on hydrolysis increases. The effects are attributed to change in the configurational and vibrational partition functions as one covalent bond in sucrose breaks to form two molecules, which then individually form additional hydrogen bonds and alter the water's structure in the solution. Cp of the solution increases with temperature less rapidly before sucrose hydrolysis than after it. This may reflect an increase in the configurational contribution to Cp as the hydrogen bond population changes.

Introduction  引言
Kinetics of hydrolysis of sucrose to fructose and glucose in a dilute HCl solution has had an important role in the development of the theory of reaction kinetics, 1 and it has been studied by a variety of techniques. 2-6 The reaction is of first order and its conversion rate constant follows the Arrhenius equation, k = A exp(−Ea/RT), where A is a constant, Ea the activation energy, R the gas constant and T the temperature. But it has been debated whether Ea changes with T or whether the Arrhenius equation takes the form, k = ATc exp(−Ea/RT), where c is a constant, a subject discussed by Buchanan et al.6 The thermodynamics of sucrose hydrolysis has been investigated since 1920. 7-11 Recently, Goldberg et al. 12 determined the equilibrium constant of the reaction, sucrose (aqueous) +H2O (liquid) = fructose (aqueous) + glucose (aqueous) by combining the Gibbs energy changes of the reaction with the sugar phosphates at 298 K. They also determined the heat capacity change and molar enthalpy of sucrose hydrolysis in a sodium acetate buffer solution containing β-fructofuranoside at 298−316 K.12
蔗糖在稀盐酸溶液中被水解为果糖和葡萄糖的反应动力学，对反应动力学理论的发展起到了重要作用。这一反应已通过多种方法进行了研究。该反应为一级反应，其转化率常数遵循阿伦尼乌斯方程：k = A exp(−Ea/RT)，其中 A 为常数，Ea 为活化能，R 为气体常数，T 为温度。不过，关于活化能是否随温度变化，或者阿伦尼乌斯方程是否应表示为 k = ATc exp(−Ea/RT)（其中 c 为常数）这一问题，目前仍有争议，Buchanan 等人对此进行了探讨。自 1920 年以来，人们一直致力于研究蔗糖水解的热力学性质。最近，Goldberg 等人通过结合 298 K 时反应的吉布斯能量变化以及糖磷酸盐的相关数据，确定了反应蔗糖（水溶液）+ H2O（液态）= 果糖（水溶液）+ 葡萄糖（水溶液）的平衡常数。他们还在 298–316 K 范围内，测定了蔗糖在水醋酸钠缓冲溶液中水解时的热容变化和摩尔焓变。
Sucrose contains two monosaccharides, α-glucose and fructose, joined by a glycosidic bond between carbon atom 1 of the glucose unit and carbon atom 2 of the fructose unit. Its hydrolysis to fructose and glucose in a dilute HCl solution may occur partly in the human stomach that may be accelerated at high temperatures during certain health conditions. The hydrolysis reaction is exothermic, which means that the structure of the solution containing fructose and glucose in a hydrogen bonded state with H2O and HCl has a lower enthalpy than the structure of the solution containing sucrose alone. However, the entropy is expected to be higher and its value has been estimated from the knowledge of the Gibbs energy and enthalpy of the reaction.7 As part of our investigations in developing calorimetric techniques for various measurements, we have studied hydrolysis of 17 wt % sucrose in dilute HCl solution 13 at different temperatures by measuring the enthalpy decrease and heat capacity value simultaneously during the hydrolysis reaction's progress in real time.
蔗糖由两种单糖组成：α-葡萄糖和果糖。它们通过糖苷键连接在一起，该糖苷键的形成发生在葡萄糖分子的 1 号碳原子与果糖分子的 2 号碳原子之间。在稀盐酸溶液中，蔗糖可以水解为果糖和葡萄糖。这一过程部分发生在人的胃中；在某些健康状况下，高温会加速这一反应。水解反应是放热反应，这意味着，含有果糖、葡萄糖以及与 H2O 和 HCl 形成氢键的溶液，其焓值低于仅含有蔗糖的溶液的焓值。不过，该溶液的熵值则可能更高，其数值是根据反应的吉布斯能和焓值计算得出的。作为我们开发各种热量测定技术的研究的一部分，我们研究了在 13 种不同温度下，17%重量的蔗糖在稀盐酸溶液中的水解过程。我们通过实时测量水解过程中焓值的变化和热容值来研究这一反应。
Experimental Methods  实验方法
Sucrose (C12H22O11, molar mass 342.3, density 1.587 g/mL, and solubility 211.5 g/100 mL water at 298 K), was purchased from J. T. Baker and the 0.1 N HCl solution from Franceschi s.a.s., Pisa. Accurately weighed amounts of sucrose and 0.1 HCl solutions were mixed in a proportion such as to obtain 17% aqueous solution by weight, i.e., 17 g of sucrose in 83 g of the 0.1 HCl aqueous solution, or 0.6 mol of sucrose in 1 kg of final solution. The solution was mechanically shaken vigorously for a few seconds at room temperature to allow complete mixing.
蔗糖（化学式：C12H22O11，摩尔质量：342.3，密度：1.587 克/毫升；在 298 开尔文条件下，其在水中的溶解度为 211.5 克/100 毫升）是从 J. T. Baker 公司购买的。而 0.1 N 的盐酸溶液则来自 Pisa 的 Franceschi s.a.s.公司。我们精确地称量了所需量的蔗糖和 0.1 N 盐酸溶液，将其混合在一起，使得最终得到的水溶液中蔗糖的重量占比为 17%。具体来说，就是在 83 克的 0.1 N 盐酸溶液中加入 17 克的蔗糖，相当于在 1 千克的最终溶液中含有 0.6 摩尔的蔗糖。混合完成后，将溶液在室温下剧烈摇晃几秒钟，以确保充分混合。
In the temperature-modulated scanning calorimetry (TMSC), a sinusoidal temperature modulation of predetermined amplitude and frequency is superposed on the temperature-time profile of controlled rate heating and cooling of a sample. When used in an isothermal mode, the modulation is superimposed on the constant temperature profile, and the mean temperature of oscillation is kept constant. It allows one to determine the real and imaginary components, Cp‘ and Cp‘ ‘, of the complex heat capacity, Cp* = Cp‘ − iCp‘ ‘, where i = . The modulated adiabatic and scanning calorimeter used here has been described  earlier. 14 The calorimeter has been used to study denaturation of lysozyme, 15 thermodynamics of isomerization of fructose, 16 thermodynamics of polymerization, 17 decrease in the heat capacity, Cp, on structural relaxation of a polymer, 18 endothermic freezing and exothermic melting of an α-cyclodextrin, 4-methylpyridine, and water mixture, 19 formation of an ice clathrate, 20 position-dependent energy of water molecules in nanopores, 21 and relaxation in the ultraviscous state of a pharmaceutical, acetaminophen. 22 It was calibrated by using two water samples of different mass and known Cp, and the absolute Cp of the solution was determined.
在温度调制扫描量热法中，人们会在样品受控速率加热或冷却过程中的温度-时间曲线上，叠加一个具有预定幅度和频率的正弦波形温度变化。当在等温模式下使用时，该调制信号会被叠加在恒定温度曲线上，从而确保振荡过程中的平均温度保持不变。通过这种方法，可以确定复数热容 Cp* = Cp‘ − iCp‘‘的实部和虚部，其中 i 为虚数单位。此处所使用的调制式绝热扫描量热仪之前已有相关描述。该量热仪已被用于研究溶菌酶的变性过程、果糖异构化的热力学性质、聚合物聚合反应的热力学过程、聚合物结构松弛过程中热容 Cp 的下降现象、α-环糊精、4-甲基吡啶和水混合物的吸热凝固与放热熔化过程、冰笼合物的形成过程、纳米孔中水分子的能量分布情况，以及解热药对乙酰氨基酚在超粘稠状态下的松弛过程。该量热仪通过使用两种质量不同、热容已知的纯水样品进行校准，从而确定了溶液的绝对热容值。
The advantage of the TMSC technique is that it allows one to measure simultaneously both the heat released (or absorbed) and Cp as a function of time in an isothermal mode, or as a function of both temperature and time in a scanning mode. The heat flow, dH/dt, that is averaged over each period of temperature modulation yields the heat released (or absorbed) and the oscillating component of the heat flow in response to temperature modulation yields Cp. These two quantities were measured with a sensitivity of 1 μW and 0.2 mJ/K and reproducibility of 10 μW and 2 mJ/K, respectively, which leads to a Cp measurements accuracy of better than 0.2%. The baseline reproducibility in successive isothermal and scanning experiments was greater than this, and it caused uncertainty in the reproducibility of the Cp curves, but its effect on the difference between the Cp measured in the same run at two points was ∼0.03%.
TMSC 技术的优点在于：它能够以等温模式同时测量随时间变化而释放或吸收的热量，或者以扫描模式同时测量随温度和时间变化而产生的热量。通过对每个温度调节周期内的热流 dH/dt 进行平均处理，就可以得到所释放或吸收的热量；而热流中因温度变化而产生的波动部分则可以用来确定 Cp 值。这两种量的测量灵敏度分别为 1 微瓦和 0.2 毫焦/开尔文，重复性则分别为 10 微瓦和 2 毫焦/开尔文。因此，Cp 的测量精度可达到 0.2%以上。在连续的等温和扫描实验中，基线的重复性更高，不过这会带来 Cp 值测量结果的不确定性。不过，这种不确定性对在同一实验中、不同位置测得的 Cp 值之间的差异的影响仅为约 0.03%。
As in the earlier studies,15-22 approximately 250 mg of the solution was pipeted into a 2.2 mm internal diameter, 90 mm long Pyrex tube which was immediately sealed hermetically by fusing its open end by a microflame, and then inserted into the calorimeter cell already maintained isothermally at the desired temperature. This operation took about 5 min. Thereafter, thermal equilibration occurred within 900 s, corresponding to three periods of temperature modulation, during which the data collected were deemed unreliable.
与之前的研究 15-22 类似，大约 250 毫克的溶液被注入到内径为 2.2 毫米、长度为 90 毫米的 Pyrex 玻璃管中。随后，玻璃管的开口端被用微火焰密封起来，确保其完全密封。之后，该玻璃管被放入已维持在所需温度下的量热池中。整个操作过程大约需要 5 分钟。之后，系统在 900 秒内达到热平衡状态。在这 900 秒期间，由于温度不断波动，所收集的数据被认为不可靠。
A temperature-modulation frequency of 3.33 mHz (modulation period 300 s) and a peak-to-peak amplitude of 1 K were used. Two kinds of measurements were performed, each time on a new sample. In the first, a new sample was kept isothermally at each of the five temperatures 300, 305, 310, 315, and 320 K. In the second, the sample's temperature was changed between 270 and 350 K at 12 K/h rate during its heating, cooling, and second heating. Although the sucrose solution is in a chemically metastable and kinetically unstable state, its viscosity is only a few centipoises, and therefore it remains at a thermodynamic equilibrium during the modulation period at all temperatures. Therefore, Cp‘ ‘ = 0, and the measured Cp‘ is equal to Cp. Experiments were continued until a time well after the heat signal became constant, thus ensuring that the hydrolysis reaction was complete.
实验中，所使用的温度调制频率为 3.33 兆赫兹（调制周期为 300 秒），峰峰值幅度为 1 开尔文。实验分为两种类型，每种类型都针对不同的样品进行。在第一种实验中，将样品分别保持在 300、305、310、315 和 320 开尔文的五个不同温度下。在第二种实验中，样品的温度在 270 到 350 开尔文之间变化，升温/降温的速率均为 12 开尔文/小时。虽然蔗糖溶液处于化学上不稳定、动力学上也不稳定的状态，但其粘度仅为几厘泊，因此在整个调制过程中，样品在所有温度下都能保持热力学平衡。因此，Cp’=0，实际测得的 Cp’值实际上就等于 Cp。实验一直持续到热信号稳定下来之后，从而确保了水解反应已经完全完成。

These values are for the thermodynamic equilibrium state of the solution, although the state is kinetically unstable due to the continuous conversion of sucrose to fructose and glucose. A second set of experiments was performed in the TMSC mode during heating of the solutions from 270 to 350 K at 12 K/h (0.2 K/min) rate and thereafter cooling it at the same rate. In this case when dH/dt is measured with increasing T, the results contain the effects of, (i) increasing T on the molar heat of hydrolysis and (ii) increasing t on the shape of the dH/dt against T plot. But when dH/dt is measured on cooling and thereafter reheating the sample, only the effect of change in T on the hydrolyzed, chemically stable solution is measured. From these measurements, we determined the apparent heat capacity, Cp,app = (dH/dt)/q, where q = (dT/dt) is the heating rate. (Note that Cp,app is the sum of the thermodynamic or intrinsic Cp of the solution and contribution from the hydrolysis exotherm.) The overall change in H and in Cp on sucrose hydrolysis may be divided into several thermodynamic steps:  (i) the heat of solution of 1 mol of sucrose, (ii) loss of 1 mol of water from the solution, and (iii) heats of solution of 1 mol of fructose plus 1 mol of glucose in water. The enthalpy and the heat capacity change would then be equal to the sum of the thermodynamic steps i and ii minus the change of step iii. In molecular terms, hydrolysis of sucrose involves (a) breaking of a covalent bond in sucrose and hence formation of fructose and glucose, (b) a change in the number of hydrogen bonds between fructose and H2O and between glucose and H2O, and (c) a change in the hydrogen bond equilibrium between H2O and HCl and between H2O and H2O. Process a is an exothermic process, and while hydrogen bond breaking is endothermic, it is not certain whether processes b and c are exothermic or endothermic. Nevertheless, the number of hydrogen bonds in the solution after hydrolysis would differ from those before. If sucrose hydrolysis occurred in the presence of enzymes, as is considered in metabolism of fructose and glucose in the intestinal tract of mammals, its hydrolysis would have the same effect on the hydrogen bond population. This means that the enthalpy of sucrose conversion to fructose and glucose may not be reliably calculated from heat of formation of these three substances alone, and that its value would vary with the concentration of these components and of HCl in the solution. This later aspect needs to be investigated by varying the HCl concentration.
在蔗糖水解过程中，H 和 Cp 的总变化可以分解为几个热力学步骤：(i) 1 摩尔蔗糖的溶解热；(ii) 溶液中 1 摩尔水的蒸发所释放的热量；(iii) 1 摩尔果糖和 1 摩尔葡萄糖在水中溶解时所释放的热量。因此，焓和热容的变化量等于步骤 i 和 ii 所释放的热量之和，减去步骤 iii 所释放的热量。从分子层面来看，蔗糖的水解过程包括：(a) 蔗糖中共价键的断裂，从而生成果糖和葡萄糖；(b) 果糖与 H2O、葡萄糖与 H2O 之间的氢键数量发生变化；(c) H2O 与 HCl、H2O 与 H2O 之间的氢键平衡发生变化。步骤 a 是放热过程，而氢键的断裂则是吸热过程。不过，步骤 b 和 c 究竟是放热还是吸热则尚不确定。无论如何，水解后溶液中的氢键数量肯定与水解前有所不同。如果蔗糖的水解是在酶的作用下进行的，就像哺乳动物肠道中果糖和葡萄糖的代谢过程那样，那么这种水解过程会对氢键的分布产生同样的影响。这意味着，蔗糖转化为果糖和葡萄糖时的焓变，无法仅通过这三种物质的生成热来准确计算。此外，这一数值还会受到溶液中这些成分以及 HCl 浓度的变化而有所波动。因此，有必要通过改变 HCl 的浓度来进一步研究这一现象。
It is remarkable that Cp increases when a reaction is exothermic. It means that while H decreases, the true dH/dT increases. Generally speaking, a liquid's Cp has two contributions, vibrational and configurational, and it increases when  hydrogen bonds are broken and the number of accessible configurations increases, as occurs on the melting of ice, and on increasing the temperature of water above 280 K. The increase in Cp on sucrose hydrolysis to fructose and glucose may be due to an increase in both the vibrational and configurational contributions, and which one dominates is uncertain. Cp of some ultraviscous liquids increases with increase in T, as for the P type (dCp/dT > 0) liquids (orthoterphenyl and diethyl phthalate), and for some others Cp decreases with increase in T, as for the N-type (dCp/dT < 0) liquids (selenium, 1-propanol). Here dCp/dT is positive for both the sucrose solution and its hydrolyzed state, but its magnitude is greater for the hydrolyzed state, thereby showing a relatively higher temperature sensitivity to the structure of the hydrolyzed state.
值得注意的是，当反应为放热反应时，Cp 会增大。这意味着，虽然 H 值减小，但真正的 dH/dT 值却有所增加。一般来说，液体的 Cp 由振动能和构型能两部分构成。当氢键被打破、可获得的构型数量增加时，Cp 就会上升。例如，在冰融化时，以及水的温度高于 280 K 时，都会出现这种情况。蔗糖水解为果糖和葡萄糖时，Cp 的上升可能是由于振动能和构型能的共同作用所致，具体哪一种因素起主导作用则尚不确定。对于某些超高粘度液体，随着温度的升高，Cp 也会增加，这类液体属于 P 型液体（dCp/dT > 0），例如邻甲苯基和邻苯二甲酸二乙酯。而另一些液体则属于 N 型液体（dCp/dT < 0），随着温度的升高，Cp 反而会下降，例如硒和 1-丙醇。在蔗糖溶液及其水解产物中，dCp/dT 的值都是正的，不过水解产物的这一数值更大，这说明水解产物的结构对温度的变化更为敏感。

Conclusions  结论
Simultaneous measurements of the enthalpy-change and of Cp performed in real time show that the temperature modulated calorimetry is a useful technique for determining the kinetic rate constants and thermodynamics of chemical reactions. A further advantage of the method is that measurements of the rate kinetics may be made rapidly during the heating of the solution at a fixed rate. The first-order reaction kinetics for sucrose (aqueous) → fructose (aqueous) + glucose (aqueous) in HCl solution has an activation energy of 109.2 kJ/mol of sucrose. The enthalpy of conversion is 14.4 kJ/mol at 310 K, and the increase in the heat capacity is 61 J mol-1 K-1 of sucrose. The lower enthalpy and entropy state formed after hydrolysis has a higher Cp. It is argued that thermodynamics of hydrolysis involves a significant contribution from a change in the hydrogen bond concentration and a possible change in the structure of water, which should effect its value when components of the solution are varied.
对焓变和 Cp 的实时测量结果表明，温度调制量热法是一种用于确定化学反应的动力学速率常数和热力学性质的有效方法。该方法的另一个优点是：在以恒定速率加热溶液的过程中，可以快速测定反应的动力学参数。在 HCl 溶液中，蔗糖水解为果糖和葡萄糖的一级反应的活化能为 109.2 千焦/摩尔蔗糖。在 310 K 时，反应的焓变值为 14.4 千焦/摩尔蔗糖，而蔗糖的比热容增加了 61 焦/摩尔·开尔文。水解后生成的低焓、低熵状态对应的 Cp 值较高。有观点认为，水解反应的热力学性质受到氢键浓度变化以及水分子结构变化的显著影响，而这些因素又会随着溶液中各组分的比例变化而发生变化。
